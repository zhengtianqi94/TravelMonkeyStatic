About Us

TravelMonkey, the NEU's largest travel site, enables travelers to unleash the full potential of every trip.

TravelMonkey provides travelers with the wisdom of the crowds to help them decide where to stay, how to fly, what to do and where to eat.

TravelMonkey also lets users share their travelling stories with others, you can also search others' stories and make friends with them.

TravelMonkey site is based on Spring MVC and Bootstrap. Many advanced front-end skills have been applied on it.
